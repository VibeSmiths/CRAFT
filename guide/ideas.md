# Ideas

The Ideas panel is where content starts. Generate ideas with AI, capture inspiration from YouTube, or add your own manually.

## Creating Ideas

### Manual

1. Click the **expand/add** area at the top of the Ideas panel
2. Enter a **Title**, **Hook** (what grabs the viewer), and **Angle** (your unique take)
3. Select **Short** or **Long** format
4. Add tags and click **Save**

### AI Generate

1. Click **Generate Ideas** (or the AI button on mobile)
2. Optionally add context (e.g., "trending topic: James Webb images")
3. Select your AI model
4. Click **Generate 5 Ideas**

The AI generates ideas in your channel's character voice, with titles, hooks, angles, and tags.

### From Discover

When researching YouTube videos in the [Discover](/guide/discover) panel, click **Save as Idea** on any video. The idea captures the video's metadata (views, duration, description) as enrichment data.

## Managing Ideas

### Search & Filter

- **Search** — filter by title, hook, angle, or tags
- **Type toggle** — All / Short / Long
- **Sort** — Newest, Oldest, Title
- **Hide converted** — toggle to show/hide ideas already converted to scripts

### Editing

Click an idea to expand it. Edit title, hook, angle, tags, and type inline.

### Deleting

- **Web** — multi-select with checkboxes, then bulk delete
- **Mobile** — long-press an idea card for delete confirmation

## Convert to Script

When an idea is ready to develop:

1. Expand the idea
2. Click **Convert to Script**
3. A new script is created with the idea's title, hook, angle, and any enrichment data (transcripts, stats from Discover)
4. The idea is marked as converted and hidden by default

::: info Source Tracking
Every idea tracks its source: `manual`, `ai`, or `discover`. Ideas from Discover carry metadata through to scripts for reference.
:::
